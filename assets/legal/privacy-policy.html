Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

At Layers ("we," "us," or "our"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Collected when you create an account to authenticate you and communicate important updates.
  • User ID: A unique identifier generated to link your account with your projects and purchases.

Content You Provide

  • Images: Photos and images you upload for layer extraction processing.
  • Extracted Layers: AI-processed images with separated backgrounds and subjects.
  • Project Metadata: Project names, creation dates, and export formats you choose.

Usage Data

  • App interaction analytics (feature usage, errors)
  • Device information (OS version, device model)
  • Purchase and subscription status

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To process your images and deliver extracted layers.
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and manage your account.
  • AI Processing: Images are sent to fal.ai (via BuildShip) for AI-powered layer extraction using BiRefNet technology.
  • Storage: Your projects and extracted layers are stored securely in Supabase cloud storage.
  • Communication: To send important service updates and respond to support requests.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our App.

Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored in secure cloud infrastructure (Supabase)
  • Images are encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Authentication is handled via secure JWT tokens
  • We implement Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure you can only access your own data

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our App:

  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and file storage
  • BuildShip: Backend workflow automation for image processing
  • fal.ai: AI-powered image segmentation (BiRefNet model)
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase and subscription management
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics to improve the App

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We only share the minimum data necessary for them to provide their services.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your projects and exported layers at any time.
  • Delete: Delete individual projects or your entire account.
  • Export: Download your layers in PNG or PSD format.
  • Account Deletion: Permanently delete your account and all associated data from within the App settings.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • All your projects and extracted layers are permanently deleted
  • Your email and user profile are removed from our systems
  • Purchase history may be retained for legal/tax purposes but anonymized

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
