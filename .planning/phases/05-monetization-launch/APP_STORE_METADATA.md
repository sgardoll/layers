## Description

Transform any image into editable transparent layers with the power of AI.

Layers uses advanced AI technology to automatically separate your images into distinct, transparent layers — perfect for designers, artists, and creative professionals who need quick access to individual image elements.

**KEY FEATURES**

• AI-Powered Separation — Upload any image and watch as AI intelligently identifies and extracts separate elements into transparent layers

• 3D Layer Viewer — Visualize your layers in an immersive 3D "cards in space" view, making it easy to understand layer depth and relationships

• Full Layer Control — Adjust visibility, opacity, position, rotation, and scale of each layer independently

• Export Options — Download individual PNG layers, bundled ZIP files, or specialized layer packs for your creative workflow

• Cloud Sync — Your projects are automatically saved and accessible across all your devices

**PERFECT FOR**

• Graphic designers extracting elements from photos
• Digital artists creating collages and compositions  
• Social media creators making eye-catching content
• Anyone who needs transparent background images fast

**SUBSCRIPTION**

Layers offers a free tier with 3 projects. Upgrade to Layers Pro for unlimited projects:
• Monthly: $4.99/month
• Yearly: $49.99/year (save 17%)

Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings.
